My Journey

From Hobby to Passion: My Cosplay Story

Cosplay first came into my life when I was sixteen, and at the time it was just something fun I wanted to try. I never imagined it would grow into such a big part part of who I am today. Over the years I've spent countless hours sewing, crafting, and learning new skills, and while it can be challenging, the end results always make the effort worthwhile. What started as a simple hobby has become a passion that continues to inspire me, push me creatively, and connect me with an amazing community of people who share the same love for bringing characters to life.

As I grew more confident in my cosplays, I realized that what I enjoy most is not just wearing the costume but also sharing what I've learned along the way. Every mistake, every late-night fix, and every small victory has taught me something valuable. Now I want to pass that knowledge on to others who are just starting out, so they can feel encouraged, avoid some of the struggles I faced, and discover the same joy that made me fall in love with cosplay.

The stages of my cosplay journey so far:

  1. Starting small - experimenting with simple costumes and learning the basics.
  2. Developing Skills - myself sewing, prop-making, and makeup techniques.
  3. Overcoming Challenges - time, budget, and the struggles of getting things just right.
  4. Sharing my work - experiencing the excitement of conventions and online communities.
  5. Giving backwanting to share my knowledge so others can enjoy the journey too.
